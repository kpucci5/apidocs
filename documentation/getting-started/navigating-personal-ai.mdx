---
title: "Navigating Personal AI"
---


Personal AI's interface is divided into different areas.

1. **Sidebar** - access your AI personas, messages, and channels.

2. **Top bar** - search and access help


![Main Interface](/images/navigate1.png)

- The My AI tab leads to My Personas, Pinned Personas, and Shared Personas.

<img
  src="/images/navigate2.png"
  alt="Main Interface"
  width="50"
  height="200"
/>


- The **Messages** tab leads to DMs and Channels.


<img
  src="/images/navigate3.png"
  alt="Main Interface"
  width="50"
  height="200"
/>

- In the **Messages** tab, the **All** section displays both DMs and Channels in a single list.


![Main Interface](/images/navigate4.png)


- The **DMs** tab includes both Persona DMs and direct messages with other members in the workspace.


![Main Interface](/images/navigate5.png)

- The **Channels** tab includes both shared and private channels. You can also favorite channels to pin them to the top of the list for easy access.
