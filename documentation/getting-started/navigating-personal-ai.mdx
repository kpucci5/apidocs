Main Interface of Personal AI

### Personal AI's interface is divided into 4 areas:

1. **Sidebar** - access your AI personas, messages, channels, and AI tools.

2. **Top bar** - search and access help

3. **AI details pane** - displays a list of your AI personas or settings for your AI persona

4. **Main Pane** - displays insights on memories and messages, chat history for DMs, channels and chats with your AI personas

**My AI -** access the AI personas in your workspace.

**Messages** - access your DMs and channels and create new ones

**AI Tools** - access a suite of LLMs like ChatGPT or Claude and custom PLMs

## Top Bar

From the top bar, you can collapse the sidebar and AI details pane, run a global search of your AIs memories and messages, and access help resources

  className="block dark:hidden"
  src="/images/top-bar.png"

## AI details Pane

When you select **My AI**, the details pane will display a list of your AI personas. Simply navigate to the desired persona or click on the plus âž• icon to create a new persona.

<img
  className="block dark:hidden"
  src="/images/ai-details-pane.png"
  alt="AI Details Pane"
/>

When you select **Messages**, the details pane will display a list of your channels and DMs. Simply navigate to the desired channel or DM or click on the pencil icon to create a new message or channel.

Click on the three-dot icon to manage your DMs and channels. From here you can set channel details, manage members, favorite DMs or channels, and leave or delete a channel.

<img
  className="block dark:hidden"
  src="/images/messages.png"
  alt="Persona Settings Light"
/>

## Main Pane

When you select a persona, message, or channel from the AI details pane, you'll see the chat history in the main pane. This is also where you'll see search results, document editor, and upload tools.
