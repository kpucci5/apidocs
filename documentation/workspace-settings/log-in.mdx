title: How To Log In To Your Workspace
---

**Login Instructions**

**Step 1.** Go to app.personal.ai on your internet browser

**Step 2.** Enable pop-up blockers on your browser. You must accept the pop-up from Personal AI to complete the login process.

How To Enable Pop-Ups
- Instructions for [Google Chrome](https://support.google.com/chrome/answer/95472?hl=en&co=GENIE.Platform%3DDesktop)
- Instructions for [Safari](https://support.apple.com/en-ca/guide/safari/sfri40696/mac)
- Instructions for [Edge](https://support.microsoft.com/en-us/microsoft-edge/block-pop-ups-in-microsoft-edge-1d8ba4f8-f385-9a0b-e944-aa47339b6bb5)
- Instructions for [Firefox](https://support.mozilla.org/en-US/kb/pop-blocker-settings-exceptions-troubleshooting)

**Step 3.** When you enter your email and input the 6-digit code, you will land on a page that has the workspace you belong to. Click "Join" to join the [Your Company] workspace and work with your team personas. 

**Step 4.** After joining your workspace, you'll be prompted to sign in once more - enter your email again and use the new 6-digit code sent to you.

**Step 5.** When joining your workspace you will be prompted to set up your profile. This is a one-time set-up and the last step of the sign-in process.
