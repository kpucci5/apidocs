---
title: "Delete Owner Account"
---

If an **Owner** wants to delete their account, they can do so from **Profile Settings** under **Personal Settings**.

![Profile Settings](/images/deleteowner1.png)

- Before deleting the account, a confirmation popup will appear, informing you that ownership must be transferred to another user first.

![Delete Account](/images/deleteowner2.png)

- Once the **Owner** transfers ownership, they will become an **Admin** and can then proceed to delete their account. After deletion, they will be redirected to the login page.

![Transfer Ownership](/images/deleteowner3.png)
