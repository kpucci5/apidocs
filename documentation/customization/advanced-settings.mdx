---
title: "Advanced Settings"
---

The Advanced Settings allows you to clear your AI persona's memories or delete the persona entirely.

<img alt="Persona Settings Light" classname="block dark:hidden" src="/images/Advanced Settings.png" />

Note, these actions cannot be undone.

**Clear memories**
Clear memories clear **all** memories associated with that particular AI persona.

**Delete AI Persona**
Delete memories clears all memories associated with that particular AI persona.
