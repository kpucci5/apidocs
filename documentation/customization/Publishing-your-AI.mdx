#

The **Publishing & Sharing** section allows you to control how others can interact with your AI.

<img
  className="block dark:hidden"
  src="/images/publishing-sharing.png"
  alt="Publishing & Sharing"
/>

#### **1. Privacy Settings**

* **Private**: Only you have access to your AI.
  Comment

* **Shared**: Others can request access to chat with your AI.
  Comment

* **Public**: Anyone can chat with your AI without approval.
  Comment

#### **2. Brand Page Link**

Customize and share a public link to your AI's Brand Page.&#x20;

#### **3. Brand Page**

Your AI's Brand Page is a public-facing profile for your AI. Sharing your AIs Brand Page allows your contacts to learn more about the purpose of your AI, chat with your AI (depending on your provacy settings), or join you on Personal AI. Click 'Edit' to customize what others see when visiting your AI's Brand Page.

#### **4. Brand Page Chat**

Enabling public chat on your brand page will allow other users to see the option to "Public Chat" with your AI when they go to your brand page. Learn more about [Public Chat](https://app.gitbook.com/o/nbVBaUyixnBYw8JgeVIt/s/dbMN7r709l6jIY1z4OHN/messaging-your-ai/public-chat).
