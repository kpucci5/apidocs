# Memory Integrations: Outlook


<img alt="Outlook Memory Integration" classname="block dark:hidden" src="/images/outlook-1.gif" />

### 1. How to start

#### 1.1 Connect to Your Outlook Account[](#id-1.1-connect-to-your-outlook-account)

<img alt="Connect Outlook Integration" classname="block dark:hidden" src="/images/outlook-2.png" />

* Open the Personal AI application and navigate to the Integrations page

* Look for the Outlook integration option

* Click on "Connect" to initiate the connection process

* You will be redirected to sign in to your Microsoft account

* Grant the necessary permissions for Personal AI to access your Outlook

### 2. How to upload

#### 2.1 Select Labels to Sync

<img alt="How to Upload" classname="block dark:hidden" src="/images/outlook-3.png" />

* After connecting, you'll be directed back to the integration page&#x20;

<img alt="Outlook Integration Page" classname="block dark:hidden" src="/images/outlook-4.png" />

* After clicking the "Sync from Outlook", you'll be presented with a popup for selecting Outlook labels

<img alt="Sync from Outlook" classname="block dark:hidden" src="/images/outlook-5.png" />

* Choose which labels you want to sync with Personal AI

<img alt="Labels in Outlook" classname="block dark:hidden" src="/images/outlook-6.png" />

* You can select multiple labels

* Click "Save" or "Add" to confirm your selection

<img alt="Select Multiple Files" classname="block dark:hidden" src="/images/outlook-7.png" />

* Personal AI will begin uploading emails from the selected labels

* A progress bar will indicate the status of the upload

* This process may take some time depending on the volume of emails

### 3. How to Manage

**3.1 Check the Uploaded Label**

<img alt="Uploaded Labels in Outlook" classname="block dark:hidden" src="/images/outlook-8.png" />

* Navigate to the Email View in the Outlook integration⁠⁠

* Click on the "Synced Labels" tab

* Review the uploaded labels

**3.2 Assign the file to other Personas**

<img alt="Assign File to Other Personas" classname="block dark:hidden" src="/images/outlook-9.png" />

* Navigate to the Email View in the Outlook integration⁠⁠

* Click on the "More" button at the end of card

* Select the "Assign to Personas" option⁠⁠

* A drop-down menu will appear, click “personas” you want to sync

* Support multiple select

#### 3.3 Edit Files

* Navigate to the Email View in the Outlook integration⁠⁠

* Click on the Edit button or click the File card

* The page will change to a file editor

**3.4 Delete Files**

<img alt="Delete Files in Outlook" classname="block dark:hidden" src="/images/outlook-10.png" />

* Navigate to the Email View in the Outlook integration⁠⁠

* Click on the "More" button to open the menu⁠⁠

* Select the "Delete" option⁠⁠

* A confirmation popup will appear; click "Confirm" to delete the email⁠**⁠**

### 4. Outlook Account

**4.1 Connect to Multiple Accounts**

<img alt="Connect to Multiple Outlook Accounts" classname="block dark:hidden" src="/images/outlook-11.png" />

* Click email account

* Click "Connect to a new account" on the bottom

* You will be redirected to sign in to your Microsoft account

* Grant the necessary permissions for Personal AI to access your Outlook

**4..2 Switch Account**

<img alt="Switch Outlook Account" classname="block dark:hidden" src="/images/outlook-12.png" />

* Click the account you want to switch

* The account will be replaced with the newly selected account

**4.3 Delete Account**

<img alt="Delete Outlook Account" classname="block dark:hidden" src="/images/outlook-13.png" />

* Click the garbage can icon beside the email account

* A confirmation popup will appear; click "Delete" to delete the account

### 5. Sync

#### 5.1 Manually Sync[](#id-5.1-manually-sync)

<img alt="Manually Sync Outlook" classname="block dark:hidden" src="/images/outlook-14.png" />

* Enter Synced Labels

* Click the 3-dots button (More button) to open the menu

* Select “Manually Sync Label"

#### 5.2 Auto Sync

* By default, Personal AI automatically syncs your selected Outlook label every 24 hours

### **6. Troubleshooting**

<img alt="Troubleshooting Outlook Integration" classname="block dark:hidden" src="/images/outlook-15.png" />

* If you encounter connection issues, look for a "Reconnect" button near your email account

<img alt="Reconnect Outlook" classname="block dark:hidden" src="/images/outlook-16.png" />

* If you see a warning icon near your email account, it indicates an expired background account. Click the email to open the menu and reconnect.

* For persistent problems, you may need to disconnect and reconnect your account
