The One Drive integration allows you to sync folders and upload individual files from your One Drive to your AI's memory.

<img
  className="block dark:hidden"
  src="/images/onedrive-1.gif"
  alt="OneDrive"
/>

## How to start

### 1. Connect to Your OneDrive Account

To get to the One Drive Integration screen, click on your **Persona -> Memory Integration -> OneDrive**.

<img
  className="block dark:hidden"
  src="/images/onedrive-2.png"
  alt="Connect OneDrive"
/>

* Open the Personal AI application and navigate to the Integrations page

* Look for the OneDrive integration option

* Click on "Connect" to initiate the connection process

* You will be redirected to sign in to your Microsoft account

* Grant the necessary permissions for Personal AI to access your OneDrive

## How to Upload

### 2. Select Files and Folders

<img
  className="block dark:hidden"
  src="/images/onedrive-3.png"
  alt="OneDrive Select Files and Folders"
/>

* Once connected, you'll see a file picker interface

* Browse through your OneDrive contents

* Select the files and folders you want to upload to Personal AI

* You can select multiple items and folders.

<img
  className="block dark:hidden"
  src="/images/onedrive-4.png"
  alt="Select Multiple Folders in OneDrive"
/>

* After selection, click "Upload" or a similar button to start the process

* A progress indicator will show the status of your upload

* Wait for the upload to complete - this may take some time depending on the volume of data

### 3. How to Manage

**3.1 Review the uploaded folders**

<img
  className="block dark:hidden"
  src="/images/onedrive-5.png"
  alt="Review Uploaded Folders in OneDrive"
/>

* Navigate to the Files View in the OneDrive integration⁠⁠

* Click on the "Synced Folders" tab

* Review the uploaded labels



**3.2 Assign to Persona**

<img
  className="block dark:hidden"
  src="/images/onedrive-6.png"
  alt="Assign to Persona in OneDrive"
/>

* Navigate to the Files View in the OneDrive integration⁠⁠

* Click on the "More" button at the end of the card

* Select the "Assign to Personas" option⁠⁠

* A drop-down menu will appear, click “personas” you want to sync

* Support multiple select

**3.2 Edit file**

* Navigate to the Files View in the OneDrive integration⁠⁠

* Click on the Edit button or click the File card

* The page will change to a file editor

**3.3 Delete file**

<img
  className="block dark:hidden"
  src="/images/onedrive-7.png"
  alt="Delete File in OneDrive"
/>

* Navigate to the Files View in the Outlook integration⁠⁠

* Click on the "More" button to open the menu⁠⁠

* Select the "Delete" option⁠⁠

* A confirmation popup will appear; click "Confirm" to delete the email⁠



### 4. OneDrive Account

**4.1 Add a New Account**

<img
  className="block dark:hidden"
  src="/images/onedrive-8.png"
  alt="Add New Account in OneDrive"
/>

* Click the email account on the top

* Click "Connect to a new account" on the bottom

* You will be redirected to sign in to your Microsoft account

* Grant the necessary permissions for Personal AI to access your OneDrive

**4.2 Switch the Account**

<img

  src="/images/onedrive-9.png"
  alt="Switch Account in OneDrive"
/>

* Click the account you want to switch

* The account will be replaced with the the new selected account

**4.3 Switch the Account**

/>

* Click the garbage can icon beside the email account

### 5. Sync

**5.1 Manually Sync**

<img
  className="block dark:hidden"
  src="/images/onedrive-11.png"
  alt="Manually Sync OneDrive"
/>

* Enter Synced Labels

* Click the 3-dots button (More button) to open the menu

* Select “Manually Sync Label”

**5.2 Auto Sync**

By default, Personal AI automatically syncs your uploaded OneDrive folders every 24 hours
