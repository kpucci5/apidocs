---
title: "Memory Integrations: Gmail"
---


The Gmail integration lets you sync emails directly from your email, automatically uploading data to your persona. These uploads will update dynamically as you make changes in Gmail labels.
- To connect your Gmail, click the purple **Connect** button.


![Gmail Integration](/images/g1.png)


- To log in, you'll be redirected to an authentication pop-up. Follow the prompts to complete the login process and grant access.


![Gmail Integration](/images/g2.png)


- Once connected, you'll be directed to your Gmail, where you can select the labels you'd like to sync to your persona's memory.


![Gmail Integration](/images/g3.png)


- After selecting the labels you want to sync, click **Sync** in the bottom-right corner.


![Gmail Integration](/images/g4.png)


- Once synced, the emails will begin uploading automatically to your persona.


![Gmail Integration](/images/g5.png)


If you synced a label, any new emails added to it in Gmail will be automatically uploaded to your persona.


![Gmail Integration](/images/g6.png)


In the top-right corner, you'll see timestamps showing when the last sync occurred and when the next one is scheduled.


![Gmail Integration](/images/g7.png)
