# Memory Integrations: Google Drive


The Google Drive integration allows you to sync folders and upload individual files from your Google Drive to your AI's memory.

To get to the Google Drive Integration screen, click on your **Persona -> Memory Integration -> Google Drive**.

<img
  className="block dark:hidden"
  src="/images/drive-1.gif"
  alt="Google Drive Integration"
/>

### **Connecting Google Drive**

To connect your Google Drive account, click the Connect button to begin the authentication process.

<img
  className="block dark:hidden"
  src="/images/drive-2.png"
  alt="Connecting Google Drive"
/>


You will be prompted to log into your Google account and grant necessary permissions for access.

Once connected, choose the folder(s) you want to sync or the files you want to add. New files added to the selected folder(s) in Google Drive will automatically sync and upload to the AIâ€™s memory.



To add additional files or folders, click the **Add from Google Drive** button and select the additional files or folders you would like to sync.

<img
  className="block dark:hidden"
  src="/images/drive-3.png"
  alt="Sync Folders from Google Drive"
/>

To disconnect your account or connect additional accounts, click the dropdown next to your Google Drive account at the top of the page.

<img
  className="block dark:hidden"
  src="/images/drive-4.gif"
  alt="Disconnect Google Drive"
/>

### Uploaded Files and Synced Folders

You can review files that have been uploaded and monitor which folders are synced by navigating to the Uploaded Files and Synced Folders tabs located at the bottom of the Google Drive integration page.

<img
  className="block dark:hidden"
  src="/images/drive-5.png"
  alt="Uploaded Files and Synced Folders"
/>

**Uploaded Files:** [View and manage](/o/nbVBaUyixnBYw8JgeVIt/s/dbMN7r709l6jIY1z4OHN/training-your-ai/memory-upload/managing-uploaded-data) all files that have been uploaded from Google Drive.

**Synced Folders:** Manage the folders that are currently syncing with your AI and manually sync or stop sync as needed.
