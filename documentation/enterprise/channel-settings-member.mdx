# Channel Settings for Workspace Members

### Channel Settings - Members

- **Channel Members** (users who are neither **Owners** nor **Managers**) have **view-only access** to all channel settings. They can see the list of members in the channel but cannot invite new members or make changes to settings.

![Channel Members for Members](/images/channel22.png)


### Channel Settings - Personas

- Channel Members can also view and search for **Personas** within the channel.

![Channel Personas for Members](/images/channel23.png)
