---
title: "Messaging Integrations"
---

The **Messaging Integrations** allows you to connect third-party apps for your AI to send messages from Personal AI to your other accounts.

![](image.png)

**SMS:** Clicking "Join Beta" will bring up a link to apply for the SMS messaging beta. If approved, a phone number is generated for your AI and can be used to text your AI directly.


**Zapier:** Zapier is automation tool that connects apps and services to automate tasks without needing to write code. Here you can integrate your AI with a number of different apps compatible with our API. Click "Show API Key" to view your API key. Your API key is important and is used to identify your AI as the AI the messages are going to.
