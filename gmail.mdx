The Gmail integration allows you to sync emails and labels from your GMail account to your AI's memory.

To get to the GMail Integration screen, click on your **Persona -> Memory Integration -> GMail**.

![](image.png)

How to navigate to the GMail Integration

### Connecting Gmail

To connect your Gmail account, click the Connect button to begin the authentication process. You will be prompted to log into your Google account and grant necessary permissions for access.

<img
  src="/images/hero-light.svg"
  alt="Persona Settings Light"
/>

Connecting your GMail account

Our GMail integration is in the final approval stages. If you get the screen '**Google hasn't verified this app**' you can proceed by clicking **Advanced** -> **Go to Cardon.ai** to continue with setup.

![](image.png)

Once connected, click **Sync from Gmail** then choose the label(s) you want to sync to your AI's memory. New emails added to the selected label(s) in GMail will **automatically sync** **every 12 hours** and upload to your AIâ€™s memory.

![](image.png)

To disconnect your account or connect additional accounts, click the dropdown next to your Google Drive account at the top of the page.

![](image.png)

### Uploaded Emails and Synced Labels

You can review emails that have been uploaded and monitor which labels are synced by navigating to the **Uploaded Emails** and **Synced Labels** tabs located at the bottom of the Gmail integration page.

![](image.png)

**Uploaded Emails:** [View and manage](/o/nbVBaUyixnBYw8JgeVIt/s/dbMN7r709l6jIY1z4OHN/training-your-ai/memory-upload/managing-uploaded-data) all emails that have been uploaded from Gmail.

**Synced Labels:** Manage the labels that are currently syncing with your AI and manually sync or stop sync as needed.
